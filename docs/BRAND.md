# SwiftDrop brand voice

**BOLD** — Drivers and customers need to see information instantly.

**TRUSTWORTHY** — Handling people's parcels and money requires trust.

**ENERGETIC** — Delivery is urgent; the app must feel fast and responsive.

**SOUTH AFRICAN** — Not a foreign app: familiar, accessible, and local.

Use clear hierarchy, high-contrast status badges, and confident copy. Avoid clutter; every screen should answer “what do I do next?” in one glance.
